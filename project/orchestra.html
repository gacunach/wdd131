Home

The Casablanca Youth Orchestra was formed in 2010 with just 16 students. It originally imparted just strings instruments, in 2014 thanks to the support of the town percussion was added, and then in 2018 woodwinds and brass were added to the orchestra. Now the program counts with 30 teachers of different classes and more than 200 students.

Instruments

Classes

Any instrument choice includes:

  • Instrument class: 45min of individual class with a teacher working on instrument technique and repertoire.
  • Music theory class: 1.5h of group class focusing on how to read music and ear training.
  • Orchestra sectionals: 1h of orchestra rehearsal with just the section that plays the same part.
  • Orchestra rehearsal: 3h of orchestra rehearsal.
